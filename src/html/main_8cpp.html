Setup function, executed once at the beginning of the program.

Pins 35-37, used for the LEDs, are also connected to the internal octal SPI (OSPI) interface, which is used for PSRAM. Since PSRAM is not needed in the project, it is disabled via the chip select pin on GPIO 26 to prevent side effects with other libraries. Serial communication is initialized at 115200 baud rate, and the board is initialized.
